# 📊 Pricing Strategy Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing pricing strategies to optimize revenue and understand demand behavior using data-driven techniques.
The analysis combines **Excel, Python, and Power BI** to extract insights and visualize business impact.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Identify optimal pricing strategies
* Analyze price elasticity of products
* Evaluate the impact of discounts and bundling
* Support data-driven business decisions

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Microsoft Excel** – Data cleaning & preprocessing
* **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)** – Exploratory Data Analysis
* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

* Product Name
* Unit Price
* Quantity Sold
* Revenue
* Cost Price
* Competitor Price
* Price Elasticity

---

## 🔄 Workflow

1. **Data Cleaning (Excel)**

   * Removed missing values
   * Standardized formats
   * Cleaned inconsistent entries

2. **Feature Engineering**

   * Revenue = Price × Quantity
   * Profit = Revenue – Cost
   * Profit Margin (%)
   * Price Elasticity

3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (Python)**

   * Price vs Quantity analysis
   * Elasticity identification
   * Competitor comparison

4. **Strategy Development**

   * Discount Strategy
   * Bundle Strategy
   * Premium Pricing
   * Competitive Pricing

5. **Dashboard Development (Power BI)**

   * KPI Cards
   * Scatter Plot (Price vs Quantity vs Revenue)
   * Elasticity Bar Chart
   * Competitor vs Cost Line Chart
   * Revenue Impact Table

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Elastic products benefit from discounts (10–15%)
* Bundling increases perceived value and revenue
* Premium pricing works for inelastic products
* Competitor pricing influences demand significantly

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Apply targeted discounts on price-sensitive products
* Introduce bundle offers to increase sales
* Use competitive pricing strategies
* Focus on profit optimization
